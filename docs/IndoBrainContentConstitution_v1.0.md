# IndoBrain Content Constitution v1.0

## Mission

IndoBrain teaches real Indonesian workplace communication. It is not a literal translation product.

## Golden Rule

Chinese is the single source of truth. Indonesian must preserve the complete meaning without reducing, changing, or adding meaning.

## Required Experience Structure

Every Experience contains:

1. ID
2. Chinese title
3. Bahasa Indonesia — dialogue only
4. Penjelasan — background only
5. Today's Harvest — vocabulary only
6. Navigation
7. Buttons

## Dialogue Standard

- Include only words the speaker actually says.
- Cover every semantic element in the Chinese title.
- Do not add explanation, assumptions, background, reasons, consequences, inferred events, or AI-generated context.
- Use natural, short Indonesian workplace speech.

## Penjelasan Standard

Penjelasan may explain the background, reason, scene, business context, or workflow. It is never dialogue.

## Today's Harvest Standard

- Use 3–6 useful vocabulary items, fixed expressions, or workplace phrases.
- Every item must occur directly in the displayed Indonesian dialogue.
- Never include placeholders, unrelated vocabulary, or `--`.

## Style

Prefer natural spoken Indonesian: `ya`, `dulu`, `tolong`, `sebentar`, and `nanti` where appropriate. Avoid literal or textbook translations.

## No Hallucination

Never add a time, location, meeting, traffic condition, child, customer, restaurant, bank, company, weather condition, or reason unless it appears in the Chinese title.

## Mandatory QA Before Deployment

Every Experience must pass semantic alignment, dialogue-only, explanation-only, Harvest provenance, no duplication, no placeholder, responsive layout, copy and feedback actions, navigation, production build, and production verification.

This standard applies to every existing and future IndoBrain module.
